Cristiano Ronaldo: The GOAT of Football!

FlashBacks:

Cristiano Ronaldo is widely considered as one of the greatest
footballers in the history.
His unreal dominance, legacy and hard work, truly makes
him the greatest with no doubt.
He has won many career trophies
and awards including his
iconic 5 ballon d'Or, 5 champion league
titles and 4 golden boots etc.

Early Life:

Cristiano Ronaldo was born on 5th February, 1985 in Thursday
in Madeira city of the Portugal. He was the son of Jose Dinis
Aveiro and Maria Dolores Dos Santos Aveiro. His full name was
Cristiano Ronaldo Dos Santos
Aveiro. He was born to a poor family.
His father was a gardener while his mother was a cook.
Ronaldo was deeply passionate with the football.
Eventually, he started playing so much that he started
lay his mother to play football. At the age of
11, he moved to Lisbon (capital city of Portugal) to train
sporting Lisbon. All was going well but when turned
15, because he used to play football a lot, he suffered from
a heart problem named
tachycardia (a condition that causes heart to beat too fast
even at the rest). Now, in this condition he has to
choose only one option either he has to quit football or he has
to go
to for a heart surgery which has
less % of success. But of course, without any hesitation, he
choose the second option that was to go
for a surgery and luckily that surgery went succeed and
he started playing football again.

First Apearance:

After that surgery, Ronaldo joined his first
club (sporting Lisbon) in 2002
and made his debut on 29th September and he scored
2 goals to chase that victory. Because of this performance
he would win his first trophy,
Supertaça Cândido de Oliveira
(A Portuguese Super Cup).

First Big Glory:

After a year, due to his impressive skills and talent,
club named Manchester United (An English club)
signed him for 12 million
pounds. In the league
where Manchester United play, he has to compete with
different
legends. But Ronaldo didn’t hesitate, he would
score bangers from distance
of 30 meters, leave defenders alone and put the ball
into net,
almost every time he get. At that same club,
he would go onto win
premier league (biggest league of football) titles
for 3 years in row
i.e. (2007, 2008, and 2009). In that same year 2008,
he would win his
first ballon d’Or, 1st UCL and broke the record for
winning all individual awards that season.

THE PURE DOMINATION!:

After winning trophies in England, he would sign for Real Madrid
(A Spanish Club) where he would continue to dominate. In his first 2
seasons, he would not do much at Real Madrid but exactly
after that he scored 250 plus goals in just 4 years that was unbelievable.
The highest jump in football history came in 2013 by Ronaldo
with an impressive height of 2.93 meters, that's much more than
average basketball player. Again in 2014, he would lead his
team to a glorious victory; winning champions league 2nd time
while scoring at 114th minute of the game. Then, a sudden
miracle happened ,Real Madrid would the Champions League
for 3 years (2016, 2017, and 2018) in a row, What a Dominance!
With Ronaldo winning first International Trophy(Euro) in 2016 for
Portugal which defines his captaincy. From 2018, he has won
five ballon d’Or (2008,2013,2014,2016,2017) and 4 golden boots
(2008,2011,2014,2015) and many other awards and trophies.

Short Time in Italy:

After those 9 glorious years in Spain, he moved to Italy signing for
Juventus in 2018. Even in Italy, he continued to
win trophies like seria, supercopa Italia etc. Again in 2019, he led
Portugal to another victory winning nations league against Netherlands.

Return to Home:

Then in 2021, he went to England and sign for Manchester united again.
But that period was disaster for Ronaldo as he wasn’t performing what he used to
and lost the world cup Quarter-final in 2022 against morocco.

New Path:

After that in end of 2022, he signed for al Nassr (Saudi Arabian club). When
2022/23 season started, he was performing well. He even won a trophy but that
wasn’t counted a trophy officially. He even finished as
a top scorer of the season scoring 54 goals ahead of youngsters at the age
of 38, that was something impossible at that age. In 2024,
he was carrying his team to finals but because of the poor team management
and performance, he lost every final he went through.

THE COMEBACK!:

After a huge downfall, he made a comeback in 2025,
by winning another international
trophy, (nation’s league) against Spain who were at
their peak, plus scoring at the final
at the age of 40, that was truly Incredible.
Now though he is 40 years old, he is scoring
goals after goals and had a brilliant
performance at this age. Currently, he is the Highest
Goal Scorer with more than 955 plus goals.

Conclusion:

His career is regarded as one of
the greatest in history. His rival,
Lionel Messi which is also a
great player, but for me Cristiano
Ronaldo is the greatest. Besides his
football master class, he is also a kind man who donates
blood for who need it. Even in the covid time, he
changed his hotel into a hospital for millions
of dollars, just to cure the disease, which shows he isn’t legend
only in the pitch, but even outside of it.
We will never truly see a footballer like
him even in the future generation. What a player!
